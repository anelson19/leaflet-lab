Does state, region, climate or spatial environment influence how many students enroll? What about cost?

Today's students and recent alumni, compared to students a few decades ago, see the cost of college exceeding the value of their education. With tuition rates increasing, some people may shy away from attending university. However, most students see college as their only means of having a well paying job.
